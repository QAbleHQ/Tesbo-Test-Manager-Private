# Code of Conduct

Tesbo Test Manager contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful, constructive, and focused on improving the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming and professional.
- Give feedback on ideas and code, not people.
- Assume good intent while staying accountable for impact.
- Respect maintainers' time and project scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption
- Publishing private information without permission
- Spam or off-topic promotion

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project community.
